Sense & Sensibility

- 1995 Film -

這是一部由英美合拍的1995年電影，由李安執導，根據珍·奧斯汀1811年的同名小說改編。李安獲選擔任本片的導演，這部分是因為他1993年的作品《喜宴》獲得了成功。本片的成功重新引起了人們對奧斯汀作品的興趣，許多類似體裁的作品因此誕生。《理性與感性》被認為是歷史上最成功的奧斯汀作品改編電影。

- Cast -

Emma Thompson as Elinor Dashwood

Kate Winslet as Marianne Dashwood

Filming Place：

Saltram House

Broughton Castle is a medieval fortified manor house in the village of Broughton which is about two miles south-west of Banbury, Oxfordshire, England. The castle was built as a manor house by Sir John de Broughton in 1300 at a location where the confluence of three streams created a natural site for a moated manor.

Filming Place：

Barton Cottage

Barton Cottage is a small home where Mrs. Dashwood and her daughters lived after being evicted from Norland Park. It is located on the outskirts of Barton Park, the estate owned by Sir John and Lady Middleton.
